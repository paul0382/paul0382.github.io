About Me

My name is Emma and I'm a student within the IMD program here at Algonquin College. I'm taking a selection of classes that are teaching me skills that should help me get a job within the design field. Coming to Algonquin was a pivot for me professionally, as I previously worked within the paddlesports industry. I felt inspired to explore myself creatively and am excited to be here. As per a book I once read,

"A creative life is an amplified life. It's a bigger life, a happier life, an expanded life, and a hell of a lot more interesting life." (Gilbert, 2015)1

Outside of school, my hobbies include:

  • Strength training
  • Yoga
  • Climbing
  • Cross-country skiing
  • Hiking
I'd like to try to create a regular exercise schedule for myself so that I'm able to maximize my time doing each activity. I also recently learned to sew and would like to continue growing this skill. In my downtime I enjoy hanging out with friends, watching movies, and reading the occasional book.

1Author: Gilbert, Elizabeth. Big Magic. 2015.
